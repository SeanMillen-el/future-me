Court Reporter

Overview

Description

Court reporters create word-for-word transcriptions at trials, depositions, administrative hearings, and other legal proceedings. Simultaneous captioners provide similar transcriptions for television or for presentations in other settings, such as press conferences and business meetings, for people who are deaf or hard of hearing.

  • Transcribing spoken or recorded speech into written form during legal proceedings
  • Ensuring the accuracy and completeness of transcripts by reviewing and editing recorded materials
  • Providing transcripts to attorneys, judges, and other parties involved in legal cases
  • Maintaining confidentiality and impartiality while performing duties
  • Occasionally providing real-time transcription services during live proceedings for immediate access to the written record.
